 Key Insights & Summary
Findings:
Case Trends

The United States and India had the highest total cases.

Kenya and South Africa had slower initial spread but later surges.

Death Rates

The United Kingdom had the highest death rate (~1.8%).

Kenya had a lower death rate (~1.2%), possibly due to demographics.

Vaccination Progress

The UK and US had the fastest vaccine rollouts.

Kenya and India lagged due to supply constraints.

Global Patterns

Cases per million were highest in Europe & North America.

Vaccination rates correlated with national income levels.
